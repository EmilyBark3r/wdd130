Start Your Next Adventure

Where to start? We here at Moonlight Trails suggest starting with simple hikes near you, an hour or more hike should be a good start. If you're looking for more of a challenge or something over night we can help you find the perfect camping locations along hike trails where you, your friends, or family can all enjoy. Locations from camping in your own tent or if you want more of a cabin type experience, we can help you find all of your desires.
